What is the difference between var and let?
let is block scoped and it cannot be redeclared whereas var is accessible anywehre inside of the funtion it is 
declared in.
What is the difference between var and const?
const cannot be redeclared or reassigned and is also block scoped
What is the difference between let and const?
let can be reassigned, const cannot
What is hoisting?
